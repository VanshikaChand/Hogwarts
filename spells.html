Spells & Charms

Discover the most iconic spells from the wizarding world — where words have power!

Expelliarmus

Type: Disarming Charm

“Expelliarmus!”

Disarms an opponent by forcing whatever they are holding to fly out of their hand.

Lumos

Type: Light Charm

“Lumos!”

Causes the tip of the caster’s wand to emit light, illuminating dark places.

Nox

Type: Counter-Charm

“Nox!”

Extinguishes the light from a wand previously lit by the Lumos spell.

Expecto Patronum

Type: Defensive Charm

“Expecto Patronum!”

Conjures a Patronus — a protective spirit that repels Dementors and Lethifolds.

Wingardium Leviosa

Type: Levitation Charm

“Wingardium Leviosa!”

Makes objects levitate and fly at the caster’s command.

Protego

Type: Shield Charm

“Protego!”

Creates an invisible magical barrier that reflects spells and physical entities.

Accio

Type: Summoning Charm

“Accio [object name]!”

Summons an object towards the caster, no matter the distance.

Obliviate

Type: Memory Charm

“Obliviate!”

Erases specific memories from an individual's mind.

Alohomora

Type: Unlocking Charm

“Alohomora!”

Unlocks doors, windows, and other locked objects magically.
