Master the art of magic

Expelliarmus

The Disarming Charm, forces the opponent to release whatever they are holding.

Lumos

Produces light from the wand tip, useful for exploring dark places.

Alohomora

Unlocks doors and other locked objects, known as the Unlocking Charm.

Expecto Patronum

Conjures a Patronus, a protective guardian that repels Dementors.

Wingardium Leviosa

Levitation Charm, used to make objects fly or float in the air.

Avada Kedavra

The Killing Curse, one of the three Unforgivable Curses. Causes instant death.
