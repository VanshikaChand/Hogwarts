About Hogwarts

Founded over a thousand years ago by Godric Gryffindor, Salazar Slytherin, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Helga Hufflepuff, Hogwarts remains the greatest magical institution in the wizarding world. Hidden within the Scottish Highlands, it is protected by ancient spells and enchantments. Every witch and wizard dreams of receiving their letter of acceptance.
