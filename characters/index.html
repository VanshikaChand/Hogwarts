Meet the Wizards, Witches, and Magical Beings

From heroes to villains, explore every corner of the wizarding world.

Heroes & Main Characters

Harry Potter

"The Boy Who Lived"

The Chosen One who survived Voldemort's curse and defeated the Dark Lord, becoming the most famous wizard of his time.

Hermione Granger

"The Brightest Witch of Her Age"

Brilliant and brave, Hermione’s intelligence and quick thinking saved Harry and Ron countless times.

Ron Weasley

"Harry’s Loyal Best Friend"

From the pure-blood Weasley family, Ron stood by Harry through thick and thin, providing humor and heart.

Albus Dumbledore

"The Greatest Wizard of Modern Times"

Headmaster of Hogwarts, wielder of the Elder Wand, and mentor to Harry. His wisdom shaped the fate of the wizarding world.

Severus Snape

"The Half-Blood Prince"

A complex figure whose love for Lily Potter made him one of the bravest wizards Harry ever knew.

Minerva McGonagall

"Stern but Fair"

Deputy Headmistress and Head of Gryffindor House. A powerful witch who fought valiantly at the Battle of Hogwarts.

Luna Lovegood

"Extraordinarily Unique"

A Ravenclaw student known for her peculiar beliefs and unwavering kindness. Her unique perspective revealed deep truths.

Neville Longbottom

"The Reluctant Hero"

Once timid and clumsy, Neville grew into a courageous leader who destroyed the final Horcrux.

Sirius Black

"The Brave Godfather"

Harry’s godfather and a member of the Order of the Phoenix, loyal to the end.

Remus Lupin

"The Werewolf Professor"

A kind-hearted and wise Defense Against the Dark Arts professor who fought bravely despite his lycanthropy.

Dark Wizards and Villains

Lord Voldemort

"The Dark Lord"

Born Tom Riddle, he became the most feared dark wizard of all time, seeking immortality and pure-blood supremacy.

Bellatrix Lestrange

"Voldemort's Most Loyal Servant"

A dangerous and sadistic Death Eater, utterly devoted to Voldemort and feared across the wizarding world.

Lucius Malfoy

"Pure-Blood Supremacist"

A wealthy and influential Death Eater who used his power to advance Voldemort's agenda while maintaining a respectable facade.

Gellert Grindelwald

"For the Greater Good"

Once Dumbledore’s closest friend, he became a dark wizard seeking domination over Muggles. Defeated by Dumbledore in 1945.

Loyal Companions and Magical Beasts

Hedwig

"Harry's Snowy Owl"

Harry’s first birthday gift and loyal companion, always by his side through his journey.

Dobby

"The Free Elf"

A house-elf who valued freedom above all. His loyalty to Harry and friends led to his heroic sacrifice.

Buckbeak

"The Hippogriff"

A proud and majestic hippogriff who bonded with Harry and was saved from execution by the Time-Turner.

Nagini

"Voldemort's Serpent"

A massive snake and one of Voldemort's Horcruxes, fiercely loyal and dangerously powerful.
