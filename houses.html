Gryffindor

"Their daring, nerve, and chivalry set Gryffindors apart"

Founder: Godric Gryffindor
House Colors: Scarlet and Gold
Animal: Lion
Element: Fire
Common Room: Gryffindor Tower
Head of House: Professor Minerva McGonagall

House Traits

Bravery Courage Daring Nerve Chivalry Determination

Gryffindor values bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, and its colors are scarlet and gold. The house was founded by Godric Gryffindor, one of the four founders of Hogwarts. Famous Gryffindors include Albus Dumbledore, Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley. Students sorted into Gryffindor are known for their courage and willingness to stand up for what is right, even in the face of danger.

Slytherin

"Those cunning folk use any means to achieve their ends"

Founder: Salazar Slytherin
House Colors: Green and Silver
Animal: Serpent
Element: Water
Common Room: Slytherin Dungeon
Head of House: Professor Severus Snape

House Traits

Ambition Cunning Leadership Resourcefulness Determination Self-Preservation

Slytherin values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal is the serpent, and its colors are green and silver. The house was founded by Salazar Slytherin. While often misunderstood as the "dark" house, Slytherin has produced many accomplished witches and wizards who are driven, ambitious, and strategic. Notable members include Severus Snape, Draco Malfoy, and Merlin himself.

Ravenclaw

"Wit beyond measure is man's greatest treasure"

Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw
House Colors: Blue and Bronze
Animal: Eagle
Element: Air
Common Room: Ravenclaw Tower
Head of House: Professor Filius Flitwick

House Traits

Intelligence Wisdom Wit Creativity Learning Acceptance

Ravenclaw values intelligence, knowledge, curiosity, creativity and wit. Its emblematic animal is the eagle, and its colors are blue and bronze. The house was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. Ravenclaws are known for their wisdom and love of learning. They value cleverness and are often eccentric. Famous Ravenclaws include Luna Lovegood, Cho Chang, and Gilderoy Lockhart.

Hufflepuff

"Those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil"

Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
House Colors: Yellow and Black
Animal: Badger
Element: Earth
Common Room: Near the Kitchens
Head of House: Professor Pomona Sprout

House Traits

Loyalty Hard Work Dedication Patience Fair Play Tolerance

Hufflepuff values hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. Its emblematic animal is the badger, and its colors are yellow and black. The house was founded by Helga Hufflepuff. Hufflepuffs are known for being just, loyal, hardworking, and accepting of everyone. They are often underestimated but produce some of the most dependable witches and wizards.
